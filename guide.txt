**IITR CONNECT, GOOGLE CHROME EXTENSION GUIDE**

-Download and Extract the zip file.
-Open Google Chrome and click on the three dots at the top-right corner.
-Then go to More tools and select Extensions.
-Click on the 'Load unpacked' button and select the 'IITR_Connect_extension' folder from your PC.
-Then, click on the chrome extension logo and select 'Time Manager'.
-Lastly, click on the coffee logo. Select the tabs and enter the time (in minutes).

 "Enjoy surfing with time management and restrict long time distractions."
